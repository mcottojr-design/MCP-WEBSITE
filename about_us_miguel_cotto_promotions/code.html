Our Story

Founded in 2005 by multi-division world champion Miguel Cotto, our company has been a cornerstone of professional boxing. From its inception, the promotion has focused on delivering elite-level competition and world-class production values to fans globally.

Under Miguel's guidance, we have transformed the landscape of boxing in the Caribbean and beyond, creating a platform where athletes can showcase their heart and skill on the biggest stages.
