Career Bio

Carlos Ortiz is a rising star in the lightweight division. Known for his explosive speed and technical precision, he has climbed the ranks under Miguel Cotto Promotions to become a top contender with a 75% knockout ratio.

Born in San Juan, Puerto Rico, Ortiz began boxing at the age of 8. His amateur career was marked by regional championships before turning professional at 19. His relentless pressure and world-class counter-punching have made him a fan favorite in the Caribbean and beyond.
